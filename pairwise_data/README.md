# **Pairwise Data**
Raw data_pairwise fitness_growth curve.xlsx is an Excel workbook containing the following tabs:
- **Figure 1:** Relative fitness of the gyrBD429N mutants compared to gyrA91S/95D gyrB429D. The gyrBD429N mutant carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrA91S/95D gyrB429D strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Figure 2:** Relative fitness of the gyrBD429N mutants with various gyrA alleles compared to its gyrA-matched parent strain. The gyrBD429N mutant carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked parent strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Figure 3:** Relative fitness of the gyrAS91F strains with various gyrA95 alleles in the presence or absence of gyrBD429N mutation. The gyrA91F strains containing various gyrA95 alleles in the presence or absence of gyrBD429N mutation carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrAS91F strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 1:** In vitro growth kinetics of isogenic GCGS0481 strains with different gyrA alleles. All isogenic strains were cultured separately in liquid GCP media supplemented with Kellogg’s supplement at a starting absorbance reading at 600nm (OD600) = 0.1. OD600 and CFU readings at 0, 2, 4, 6, and 8 hours timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 2:** In vitro growth kinetics of isogenic GCGS0481 strains with gyrBD429N in combination with different gyrA alleles. All isogenic strains were cultured separately in liquid GCP media supplemented with Kellogg’s supplement at a starting absorbance reading at 600nm (OD600) = 0.1. OD600 and CFU readings at 0, 2, 4, 6, and 8 hours timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 3:** Relative fitness of the gyrA91S/95D gyrB429D strain compared to the gyrBD429N mutants with various gyrA alleles. The gyrA91S/95D gyrB429D strain carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrBD429N mutant strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 4:** Relative fitness of the gyrA-matched parent strains compared to the gyrBD429N mutants with various gyrA alleles. The gyrA-matched parent strain carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrBD429N mutant strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 5:** Relative fitness of the isogenic gyrA mutant strains compared to the gyrA91S/95D strain. The gyrA mutant strain carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrA91S/95D strain. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 6:** Relative fitness of the isogenic gyrA91S/95D compared to the gyrA mutant strains. The gyrA91S/95D strain carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrA mutant strains. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
- **Suppl Figure 7:** Relative fitness of the gyrA91F/95D strains compared to the gyrA91F strains containing various gyrA95 alleles in the presence or absence of gyrBD429N mutation. The gyrA91F/95D strain (with or without gyrBD429N) carried a kanamycin marker and was cocultured with the unmarked gyrA91F strains containing various gyrA95 alleles in the presence or absence of gyrBD429N mutation. CFU values for both unmarked and KAN-marked plates at each timepoint are reported.
